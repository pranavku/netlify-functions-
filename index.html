Swim is your #1 limiter — here's the targeted fix

Phase 1 Swim Goal

Build to 750m open water pace at <2:00/100m before Sprint Tri on Aug 2. Focus entirely on technique for the first 6 weeks before adding endurance volume. A smooth, efficient stroke at 1:55–2:05/100m will carry you through the Sprint and serve as the base for the 70.3 1.9km swim in 2027.

Catch-up drillSide kickFingertip dragFist gloves6-1-6 kickTarzan drillBilateral breathing

Weekly Swim Structure (3×/wk)

Session 1 — Technique (Mon/Tue): 1,500–1,800m. Warm-up 400m easy, 4×100m drill focus, 4×200m controlled pace with pull buoy, 200m cool-down.

Session 2 — Endurance (Wed/Thu): 2,000–2,500m. Z2 effort, bilateral breathing enforced. Build to 3,000m by week 10.

Session 3 — Threshold (Fri/Sat): 1,800–2,000m. Warm-up + 4–6×400m at CSS pace with 30s rest. Add one rep every 2 weeks.

CSS testing protocol
Week 1: Swim 400m all-out, rest 10 min, swim 200m all-out. Record both times.
CSS pace: (400m time − 200m time) ÷ 2 = critical swim speed per 100m.
Retest: Every 6 weeks to track improvement and reset zone targets.
Threshold sets: Perform at CSS ± 3 sec/100m.
